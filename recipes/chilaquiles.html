Chilaquiles Step-by-Step Recipe

What is This Plate?

A beloved Mexican dish perfect for breakfast or brunch, chilaquiles feature crispy tortilla chips smothered in a tantalizing blend of tangy salsa and savory toppings. Garnished with fresh cilantro, diced onions, and a dollop of creamy queso fresco, these chilaquiles are a flavorful and satisfying way to start your day.
