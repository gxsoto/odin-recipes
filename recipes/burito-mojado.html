Burrito Mojado Step-by-Step Recipe

What is This Plate?

Indulge in the ultimate Mexican comfort food with these flavorful and satisfying burrito mojados. Stuffed with your choice of mouthwatering fillings and topped with a savory enchilada sauce and melted cheese, these burritos mojados are sure to be a hit at your table, offering a delightful fusion of traditional Mexican flavors.

Ingredients

  • Corn or Flour Tortillas
  • Pinto Beans
  • Cheese of Choice
  • Sour Cream
  • Marinated Chicken
  • Limes
  • Salt
  • Corn Oil
  • Tomatillos
  • Poblano Peppers
  • Garlic
  • Oregani
  • Cumin
  • Fresh Cilantro
  • Cooked Rice

The Process

Making the Tomatillo Sauce:

Roasting tomatillos and green chile peppers to perfection, their charred notes harmonize with garlic, a pinch of sea salt, and a hint of Mexican oregano. These elements converge to create a vibrant, zesty sauce that will infuse your burritos with traditional flair.

Marinating Chicken:

Infuse the meat with the earthy allure of ground cumin, the zing of fresh lime juice, and a sprinkle of sea salt. Allow the marinated chicken to soak in these authentic flavors for a minimum of an hour before skillfully grilling or pan-frying it to tender perfection, each bite offering a tantalizing blend of Mexican authenticity.

Assembling the Burrito

Gently warmed on a traditional comal or skillet, they provide the canvas for your culinary masterpiece. Begin by layering the tortilla with velvety refried pinto beans, each spoonful capturing the essence of comfort and tradition. On this foundation, arrange slices of marinated chicken, accompanied by the creamy decadence of Oaxaca cheese and a scattering of cooked rice.

Marinating the Burrito and Baking:

With meticulous care, fold the sides of the tortilla and artfully roll it into a burrito, a labor of love that encapsulates the authenticity of Mexican cuisine. Place these culinary treasures in a baking dish, where they eagerly await the transformative touch of the flavorful green chile sauce. Pour this vibrant elixir generously over the burritos, ensuring that every morsel is embraced by the beguiling symphony of tastes.

Elevate the experience of each Green Chile Burrito by allowing them to bake until warmth infuses every bite and the flavors meld harmoniously. As these creations emerge from the oven, present them on plates as a homage to the heritage they represent. Drizzle crema, the Mexican sour cream, over the burritos for a luxurious touch that enhances the indulgence. Finally, awaken the senses with a sprinkle of fresh cilantro and a burst of lime juice, brightening the ensemble with the vibrant colors and flavors of Mexico.
